Intel Sustainability Goals

A horizontal, scroll-snap timeline highlighting key targets.

2025 — Responsible Sourcing

Increase responsible sourcing across supply chain; expand renewable energy projects for fabs.

2030 — 30% Emissions Reduction

Reduce greenhouse gas emissions intensity by 30% across global operations (baseline year).

2035 — Circular Products

Design products for circularity with increased recycling and reduced material waste.

2040 — Net-Zero Operations

Achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across Intel operations through energy, efficiency, and offsets.

2050 — Climate Leadership

Demonstrate industry leadership on climate, circular economy, and sustainable innovation.
